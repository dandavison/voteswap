How does it work?

When you join voteswap you will be matched with a voter in your Facebook friends network. Your match will be someone your know personally or a friend of a friend.

You're limited to swapping with someone in your network because we think this will keep everyone honest. You can trust your friends to follow through on their pledge to swap and save the presidency.

You can propose vote swaps with several friends. We automatically rank your friends by the impact. If you're in a safe state, friends in important swing states are shown first. If you're in a swing state, then friends in very safe states are shown first.

After proposing swaps and talking to several friends, you can accept one swap. On election day we'll send you both an email to remind you who you're swapping with.
