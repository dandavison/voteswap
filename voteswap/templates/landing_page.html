How does it work?

When you join voteswap you will be matched with a compatible voter in your Facebook friends network. Your match will be someone your know personally, or a friend of a friend. We think this will keep out liars and almost everyone will follow through with their pledges.

After the election

After the election, while the surprisingly high third-party turnout is still on everyone's minds, we can start a dialogue on changing our election process to use ranked choice voting, aka instant runoff elections. This system of voting lets you write down your candidates in order, allowing votes for third parties to not take away from votes for other parties.
